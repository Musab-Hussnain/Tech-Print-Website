Who We Are

A Fit-Out Partner Built on Craft & Reliability

T&P is a full-service interior fit-out and contracting studio. We bring together designers, artisans and project managers under one roof to deliver immaculate interiors — from gypsum and joinery to complete turnkey renovations.

  • Turnkey delivery — design, build, and finishing handled end-to-end.
  • In-house trades — carpenters, painters and MEP coordinators you can trust.
  • On-time, on-budget — disciplined scheduling with transparent reporting.
