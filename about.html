Our Story

Built on Craftsmanship & Trust

For over 15 years, T&P has been turning architectural vision into reality — one meticulously finished interior at a time.

Established 2009

From a Small Workshop to a Full-Service Fit-Out Studio

T&P began as a two-person carpentry workshop with a simple belief: that beautiful interiors are the result of honest craftsmanship and disciplined project management.

Today we are a complete interior fit-out and contracting firm with an in-house team of designers, gypsum specialists, joiners, painters and MEP coordinators. We have delivered over 450 projects across residential villas, apartments, offices, retail and hospitality — each one finished to the same exacting standard we set on day one.

What hasn't changed is our obsession with detail, our respect for deadlines, and our commitment to transparent communication from first sketch to final handover.

"We don't just fit out spaces — we finish them to a standard we'd be proud to live in."— Founder & Managing Director
